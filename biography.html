About the Artist – Toni Wood

Toni Wood is a self-taught artist whose creative journey began in 2018 while taking art classes at her church. What started as a new experience quickly became a lifelong passion — a way to express her faith, love, and culture through color and movement.

Working with acrylic, pencil, and watercolor, Toni’s art radiates peace and positivity. She’s especially drawn to acrylic for its vibrant tones and quick-drying quality, which reflect her joyful, faith-filled spirit. Guided by her favorite scripture — “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me” — Toni paints from the heart, creating work that uplifts and inspires.

Her art has been featured in church exhibitions and local showcases, where her message resonates deeply: creativity is a gift from God, and it’s never too late to discover it. Each piece she creates is an invitation to see the beauty of faith, love, and perseverance.
